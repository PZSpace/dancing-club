Lindisfarne Ballroom Dancing Club

Beginner Exam

The outcome of this curriculum is for young dancers to move with increased control and learn co-ordination and confidence, and laying the foundations for them to be able to learn and master more complex dance moves later on. This curriculum helps to develop the stamina, creativity and coordination of the students, which will stay with them throughout their dance career.

Time - Twice a year (June, Dec)

Fee - 100$

Requirement - Must be a member of Beginner Class
