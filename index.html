Lindisfarne Ballroom Dancing Club

LBDC

The purpose of the Lindisfarne Ballroom Dancing Club (LBDC) is to provide adults a social atmosphere in which an interest in ballroom dancing can be promoted through instruction and practice. In addition to the adult group ballroom dance classes, the LBDC also offers swing, two-step and line dancing in their curriculum, in a social, non-competitive setting.

The LBDC is a non-profit club and operated by club volunteers. The club offers yearly dance classes (September to March), social dances year round, fun theme dances, dinner & dance events, and occasional dance events/workshops.
