Lindisfarne Ballroom Dancing Club

About the Social Dances:

The “Social Dance” is an opportunity for dancers to socialize and practice the dance steps taught at classes!

These dances are open to the general public (Non-member fees apply).

Social Dances are held by the LBDC throughout the year for fun and practice. The music is selected for practicing dance steps taught in various levels of classes, with each song identified on a screen for a suggested dance.

For LBDC Members:

  • Throughout the year a few dances will include a free lesson of a dance not normally part of the curriculum and some dances are for LBDC beginners only for the first hour.
  • If interested in helping with the Social Dances, contact the LBDC Social Dance MAL (Members-At-Large) for more information.
